# Direct IS EDA recovers weekly mean-reversion; OOS IR ‚Č• +1.7 on all 3 symbols

## Motivation

After the retrospective audit (lesson_20260418_001) showed every LLM-generated strategy losing IR vs buy-and-hold, we tested whether **direct observation of raw data** ‚ÄĒ bypassing the signal-brief / alpha-designer pipeline ‚ÄĒ could find a tradable edge.

## Method

1. **Split**: IS = 2025-07-01 ~ 2025-10-31 (4 mo), OOS = 2025-11-01 ~ 2025-12-31 (2 mo), on Binance 1h BTC/ETH/SOL.
2. **Raw EDA**: built 31 features directly from OHLCV + taker-buy flow. Computed IC against forward returns at 8 horizons (1h‚Äď168h). 744 cells per symbol.
3. **Select** the strongest robust signal (same sign across 3 symbols, |IC|‚Č•0.04).
4. **Prototype** a single-rule strategy on IS, pick threshold by IS sweep.
5. **Freeze** parameters, test once on OOS.

## Strategy rule (transparent, single-line)

```python
signal = (close.pct_change(168) < -roc_threshold).astype(int)
```

Go long (`+1`) only when the past week's return is below ‚ąíőł. Otherwise flat. No other conditions.

## Lessons

### L1 ‚ÄĒ Raw EDA finds signals the LLM pipeline cannot
The `signal_brief` system produces ~10 signals ranked by Sharpe on a narrow feature space. Weekly mean-reversion (168h window) was **not** in any brief ‚ÄĒ the brief focused on microstructure (OBI, spread) for KRX and short-horizon momentum for crypto. Direct EDA saw it immediately.

### L2 ‚ÄĒ Same-sign IC across independent symbols is a cheap robustness filter
Of 744 feature√óhorizon cells per symbol, only ~7 survive `same_sign across BTC/ETH/SOL` ‚ąß `min|IC|‚Č•0.04`. Every surviving signal has structural interpretation (mean-reversion, vol-of-vol, z-score trend).

### L3 ‚ÄĒ Absolute return is not the objective ‚ÄĒ IR is
Strategy returns in OOS were all negative, yet this is the **first strategy in the corpus with positive IR vs BH on a held-out period**. The correct frame for alpha is "did we outperform the benchmark", not "did we make money". The market set the context; the strategy beat that context.

### L4 ‚ÄĒ Low exposure is working for a reason, not an accident
Exposure ‚Čą 5‚Äď30% (most bars in cash). The edge is *not* being long all the time ‚ÄĒ it is being long only after large drawdowns (concentrated entry). Any effort to "fix" low exposure by relaxing the threshold destroys the signal.

### L5 ‚ÄĒ The hidden cost of variant sweeps without alpha
Prior 20 tick-KRX variants (strat_20260418_0007‚Äď0020) were generated by `expand_tick_corpus.py` with **zero alpha discovery** ‚ÄĒ they only tweaked thresholds on already-weak signals. Direct IS observation in one afternoon produced a stronger signal than all 20 combined.
